Tutorial

Welcome to World's Simplest Poker! This is a card game that draws on mechanics from games like Poker and War, where players are dealt a single card. The winner is decided by the higher card value.

Players begin each round by placing an ante (blind). Then, both are dealt a single card from the deck. Upon seeing their assigned card, they can decided to either Raise, Check, or Fold.

Decisions are made simultaneously and independently.

If a player chooses to raise, they must place a bet equal to that of their ante. Should both players choose the same action, the game proceeds to the showdown.

In a showdown, the player with the higher card value wins (for A/K/Q, Ace high). If one player folds, the other automatically wins the round.

The game continues for multiple rounds, with players accumulating winnings in their stacks. The objective is to maximize your total balance over time.
